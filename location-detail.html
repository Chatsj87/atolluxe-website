Project Overview

The Palms @ Thoddoo represents the pinnacle of luxury living in the Maldives, combining traditional island aesthetics with contemporary design and comfort. Situated on the pristine shores of Thoddoo Island in Alif Atoll, this exclusive collection of 24 beachfront and garden villas offers an exceptional lifestyle and investment opportunity.

Each villa has been thoughtfully designed to maximize natural light and ocean views while ensuring privacy and comfort. The architecture seamlessly blends with the natural environment, featuring sustainable materials, energy-efficient systems, and a design that honors local cultural elements.

As a property owner at The Palms, you'll enjoy exclusive access to resort-style amenities, personalized services, and rental management options that generate substantial returns while allowing for personal use throughout the year.

Location

The Palms is ideally situated on the stunning white sand beaches of Thoddoo Island, offering easy access to the island's natural attractions and amenities. Just 67 km from Male International Airport, the property is accessible via a 20-minute seaplane journey or a 1.5-hour speedboat ride.

The island's unique position provides exceptional diving and snorkeling opportunities, with vibrant coral reefs and rich marine life just steps from your villa. The central location within Alif Atoll also allows for convenient day trips to neighboring islands and exclusive resorts.
